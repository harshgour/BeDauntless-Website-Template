ABOUT US

In India, about 83% of children go to school, however, only 45% of said children are able to complete Secondary school. With there being a shortage of qualified and dedicated teachers, general awareness, and standardized testing, more than 70% of students are unaware of basic academic concepts like subtraction, writing, and effective communication, leading to a largely illiterate youth.

At Dauntless, our vision is to empower children with quality education and enable them to become literate citizens, irrespective of their economic background. Further, we strive to achieve the UN Sustainable Development Goals of No Poverty, Quality Education, Zero Hunger, Good Health & Well-being, and Climate Action. We are also working to curb the spread of Child Marriage, Child Labor and Gender Discrimination through the antidote of a literate populace.

So far, we have successfully changed over 5000 lives.
